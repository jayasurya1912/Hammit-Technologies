| Who We Are

Shaping the Future of Power with Global Expertise

Shree Hamit Power Technologies (founded in 2012) is a trusted distributor of premium electrical and industrial products. We proudly partner with global brands like ABB, C&S Electric, BCH Electric, Elmex, Minilec, Selec, Eaton, Rishabh, Socomec, Hansa Green, L&T (Lauritz Knudsen), Salzer, GIC, Esbee, and more—delivering certified solutions (ISO, TUV, UL, CE) for wires, cables, starters, switchgears, control gears, distribution components, and circuit breakers.

With over a decade of excellence, we ensure timely delivery, quality assurance, and customer-first service—making us the preferred partner for industries and projects across the region.
