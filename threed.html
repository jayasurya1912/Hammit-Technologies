BEWL (Billets Elektro Werke Pvt. Ltd.) is a trusted manufacturer and exporter of electrical connectors and components, established in 1990. With a modern manufacturing facility in Gujarat and headquarters in Mumbai, the company delivers high-quality products such as tubular cable lugs, sheet-metal lugs, copper and aluminium connectors, split bolts, butt connectors, and customized parts. Their wide product range caters to the needs of industries requiring durable and reliable electrical connections

The brand emphasizes global standards, innovation, and strong quality assurance, which has helped it build a strong presence both in India and international markets. Backed by certifications and a commitment to excellence, BEWL continues to serve industries with dependable solutions, ensuring safety, efficiency, and long-term performance in electrical connectivity.

PRODUCTS

Tubular Lugs

Durable and high-conductivity lugs designed for reliable cable terminations. Widely used in electrical installations for secure connections.

Sheet Metal Lugs

Economical and easy-to-install lugs with insulation sleeves for safe wiring. Ideal for domestic and light industrial applications.

Connectors

Precision-engineered connectors for joining cables securely. Ensures strong electrical continuity with minimal resistance

Split Bolts

Heavy-duty split bolts for joining multiple conductors. Provide excellent mechanical strength and long-lasting performance.

Copper Semis

High-quality copper profiles and rods used in manufacturing electrical components. Known for excellent conductivity and durability.

Connector

Compact C-shaped connectors for joining parallel conductors. Provide efficient current flow with easy crimping installation.
