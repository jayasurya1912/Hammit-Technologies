WAGO

WAGO

WAGO India, a subsidiary of WAGO Germany, began operations in 1997 and became a 100% subsidiary in 2011. With a modern manufacturing facility in Vadodara and multiple sales offices, it delivers advanced interconnection, electronics, and automation solutions. Its expertise is anchored in the pioneering CAGE CLAMP® spring-pressure connection technology, trusted worldwide for safety and reliability.

Driven by innovation, WAGO brings cutting-edge technologies like screwless connectors, fieldbus-independent controllers, and IoT-enabled automation modules. Backed by strong infrastructure and a wide partner network, it ensures reliable supply and support across India’s industries. The company also plays a key role in exports, strengthening India’s position in the global automation market.

Electrical Interconnections

Maintenance-free connectors for DIN rails, PCBs, and control cabinets, ensuring consistent quality across all conductor types.

Interface Electronics

Reliable signal conditioners, relays, power supplies, and transformers designed to convert, isolate, and distribute signals efficiently.

Automation Technology

Advanced I/O systems, controllers, and software tools for seamless building automation and industrial machine control.

Connection Technology

Terminal blocks, field wiring connectors, and PCB systems offering robust and flexible connection solutions for industries.

Building Automation & Smart Modules

Railway & Infrastructure Solutions

High-performance connectors and components built for railway signaling, power distribution, and other critical infrastructure needs.
