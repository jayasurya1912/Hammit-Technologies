C & S ELECTRIC

Established in 1966, C&S Electric is one of India’s leading electrical equipment manufacturers, known for delivering reliable solutions in power generation, distribution, protection, and control. With decades of expertise, the company has grown into the country’s largest exporter of industrial distribution and control products, supporting both domestic and global markets

Driven by innovation and quality, C&S operates multiple state-of-the-art manufacturing plants and government-accredited R&D centers. Backed by strong engineering capabilities and international certifications, the company continues to create future-ready solutions that ensure efficiency, safety, and sustainability in every sector it serves

PRODUCTS

Air Circuit Breakers (ACBs)

Heavy-duty breakers designed to protect electrical circuits from overloads and short circuits. They ensure safe power distribution in industrial and commercial systems.

Miniature Circuit Breakers (MCBs)

Compact protection devices used in residential and commercial installations. They guard against overcurrent and short circuits while allowing easy reset.

Contactors

Reliable switching devices for motor control and power circuits. Built for durability, they support applications from light-duty to heavy industrial loads.

Busduct Systems (LV & MV)

Modular busbar solutions for efficient, large-scale power distribution. They reduce losses, save space, and allow flexible installation.

Modular Switches

Sleek and durable switches available in various designs. They combine safety, style, and ease of use for homes, offices, and modern infrastructure.

Protection Relays

Intelligent devices for monitoring and safeguarding feeders, motors, and transformers. They improve reliability, prevent failures, and support automation.
