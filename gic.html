GIC

GIC

GIC India, established in 1972 and headquartered in Pune, is a leading manufacturer of process control, industrial automation, and instrumentation solutions. Its product range includes digital hour meters, impulse counters, rate indicators, totalizers, PLCs, timers, monitoring relays, and power and signal transducers, all engineered to support modern automated industrial systems.

As an ISO-certified and professionally managed company, GIC integrates the entire workflow from design to delivery under one roof. With advanced manufacturing facilities, strong focus on innovation, and a commitment to consistent quality, GIC has built a global reputation for delivering reliable, high-performance instrumentation and automation products.

PRODUCTS

Digital Hour Meters & Rate Totalisers

Accurately track runtime, operation cycles, and total usage with precision. Ideal for maintenance planning and performance analysis in industrial systems

Impulse Counters (Series CR-18 & CR-26)

Reliable pulse-counting devices suitable for process control and monitoring applications. Durable and designed for high-frequency operation.

Timers & Mini PLCs

Programmable timers and mini PLCs provide flexible automation and process control. Enhance system intelligence with precise timing and logic capability.

Monitoring Relays & Signal Transducers

Protect systems with voltage, frequency, current, and earth-leakage monitoring relays. Signal transducers offer seamless conversion for accurate process measurement.

Protocol & Interface Converters / Gateways

Enable smooth communication between diverse systems and protocols. Provide reliable data routing and integration across automation architectures.

Mechanical Field Instruments (Flow, Pressure, Temperature & Level Devices)

Robust instruments—including pressure/temperature gauges and flow assemblies—designed for industrial applications. Engineered for precision, reliability, and process safety.
