INDOASIAN

IndoAsian is a trusted Indian-origin brand established in 1958, specializing in the design, manufacturing, and distribution of electrical products. With a focus on innovation, safety, and affordability, IndoAsian’s portfolio includes circuit breakers, distribution boards, switches & sockets, timers, LT panels, and more — serving both residential and industrial needs. Headquartered in Noida, India, the brand has become a household name in the electrical industry.

Over the decades, IndoAsian has expanded its footprint beyond India, building a strong presence in more than 60 countries across Africa, the Middle East, Asia-Pacific, Europe, and Oceania. With its commitment to reliable quality and customer-centric solutions, IndoAsian continues to power homes, businesses, and industries worldwide.
