SELEC

Selec Controls Pvt. Ltd., established in 2000, is a leading Indian brand specializing in industrial electrical, measurement, and automation products. Headquartered in Mumbai, the company is driven by precision, innovation, and affordability. Its extensive product range includes energy meters, multifunction meters, digital panel meters, protection relays, timers, counters, temperature controllers, process controllers, power supplies, PLCs, and HMIs, catering to diverse industrial needs

With advanced manufacturing, strong R&D, and globally certified systems, Selec has earned a trusted reputation across industries. Exporting to over 75 countries, the brand holds a strong global presence in automation, electrical protection, and process control solutions. Guided by a customer-focused approach and cutting-edge technology, Selec continues to deliver smart, reliable, and value-driven products worldwide
