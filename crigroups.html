C.R.I GROUPS

C.R.I. Groups is a global leader in fluid management solutions with over six decades of engineering excellence. The company manufactures pumps, motors, valves, IoT drives, solar pumping systems, wires, and cables—spanning more than 9,000 products. With 21 world-class manufacturing facilities and strategic acquisitions in the U.K. and Italy, C.R.I. has established its presence across 120+ countries.

Recognized for innovation and reliability, C.R.I. invests in research through its “Fludyn Advanced Technology Centre”, driving energy-efficient and IoT-enabled solutions. The company has earned multiple EEPC awards for exports and national recognition for energy conservation, while serving industries such as agriculture, power, construction, marine, and pharmaceuticals
