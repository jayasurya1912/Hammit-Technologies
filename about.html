About Us

12+ Years of Service
| Who We Are

Shaping the Future of Power with Global Expertise

Founded in 2012, Shree Hamit Power Technologies is a trusted name in the supply of electrical and industrial products. We are proud channel partners of reputed international companies such as ABB, C&S Electric, BCH Electric, Elmex, Minilec, Selec, Eaton, Rishabh, Socomec, Hansa Green, L&T (Lauritz Knudsen), Salzer, GIC, Esbee, and more. These globally recognized brands are pioneers in manufacturing high-quality electrical goods, certified by ISO, TUV, UL, and CE, ensuring innovation, safety, and durability. Our product portfolio includes wires, cables, starters, switchgears, control gears, final distribution components, and circuit breakers, catering to both industrial and domestic requirements

At Shree Hamit Power Technologies, our ultimate motto is “Reliability to Our Customer Satisfaction.” This dedication has led us to sustain steady growth, earn recognition as a best performer, and build long-term trust with our clients. With the continued patronage of industries and businesses, we aim to achieve greater heights by delivering excellence, strengthening partnerships, and ensuring customer satisfaction at every step.
