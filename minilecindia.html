MINILEC INDIA

Milec Group is a trusted Indian brand established in 1965, specializing in the design, manufacturing, and export of industrial electronic protection, control, and monitoring products. With a strong focus on safety, reliability, and innovation, Minilec’s portfolio includes motor & pump protection relays, phase-failure relays, monitoring relays, alarm annunciators, timers, level controllers, soft starters, and automation solutions. Headquartered in Pune, India, the company operates with state-of-the-art facilities, R&D centers, and globally recognized quality systems.

Over the years, Minilec has built a solid presence both in India and abroad, exporting to international markets since 1991. With its commitment to quality, advanced technology, and customer-centric solutions, the brand continues to serve power, process industries, and automation sectors, empowering businesses with reliable protection and control systems worldwide.

PRODUCTS

Phase Failure Relays

Protects motors and systems from phase loss, reversal, under-voltage, and over-voltage, ensuring smooth and safe operation.

Monitoring Relays

Advanced relays that monitor voltage, current, frequency, and other parameters to provide reliable protection and real-time system diagnostics.

Motor Protection Relays

Safeguards motors from overloads, single phasing, dry running, and other faults, enhancing performance and extending equipment life.

Pump Protection Relays

Specialized relays designed to protect pumps against conditions like dry running, overload, and phase failure for dependable operation.

Alarm Annunciators

Comprehensive annunciator systems that provide clear visual and audible alerts, ensuring quick detection and response to faults.

Timers

Precise timing devices that control processes, operations, and sequences in industrial and automation applications.

Level Controllers

Smart controllers that maintain liquid levels accurately, preventing overflow, dry running, and ensuring efficient pump operations.

Transducers

Reliable devices that convert electrical parameters such as current, voltage, frequency, or power into standardized signals for monitoring.

Soft Starters & Automation Solutions

Soft starters enable smooth motor startups while advanced automation and SCADA solutions provide efficient monitoring and control for modern industries.
