SALZER

SALZER

Salzer Electronics Limited, established in Coimbatore in 1985, has grown into a leading manufacturer of switchgear solutions including cam-operated rotary switches, load-break switches, wiring ducts, relays, and cable management systems. With four state-of-the-art manufacturing units, the company delivers products that cater to both domestic and global markets, ensuring reliable performance across industries.

Renowned for its focus on energy-efficient designs, international certifications (CE, UL, CSA, ISO 9001/14001/OHSAS), and R&D-driven innovation, Salzer has built a strong reputation in the electrical solutions industry. The brand continues to strengthen its footprint worldwide, offering safe, innovative, and high-quality products that meet evolving customer and industry needs

PRODUCTS

Medium Voltage Switchgear

Engineered for safe and reliable power distribution in critical infrastructure. Provides stability, protection, and efficient load management.

Load-Break & Photovoltaic Switches

Heavy-duty switches for safe disconnection of power circuits. Specially engineered PV switches ensure safety and efficiency in solar applications.

Wiring Ducts / Cable Ducts

High-quality ducts that keep wiring neat, secure, and organized. Enhance safety and simplify maintenance in electrical panels.

Industrial Wires & Cables

A wide range of flexible and durable wires and cables, suitable for both industrial and commercial applications. Designed for reliability and efficiency.

Modular Switches, Sockets & Regulators

Modern modular switches, sockets, and dimmers combining style with safety. Ideal for residential, commercial, and industrial installations.

Specialty Electronic Products

Includes fan regulators, USB and communication sockets, MCBs, RCCBs, and other innovative electrical accessories. Built for convenience and dependable performance.
